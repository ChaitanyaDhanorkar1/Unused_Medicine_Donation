Aim

Our aims is to provide an online platform for donating unused medicines or medicines to people in need. Users can register themselves in the program by submitting their required information.

Once registered users can donate medicines by providing accurate drug information to NGOs. The system has the power to prevent users from proposing an application for inappropriate or expired drugs. The system will keep a record of the medications offered and available. Users can apply to donate or use medications.
