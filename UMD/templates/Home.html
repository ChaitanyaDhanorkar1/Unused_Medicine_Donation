Who We Are

UMD is a movement for promoting medicine donation to the needy and aims at providing everyone a platform to donate and help others. The site was created and brought up in 2022 by the students of IIT Indore - Arvind Tomar, Arvind Yadav, Chaitanya Dhanorkar, Likhith Raj D, and Rishabh sharma. We are also thankful to our Professor and TAs, who provided us with help at every stage we got stuck. The people here work with enthusiasm to help people worldwide transact with each other at ease. We help millions of people to attain the help they require at the most needed hour. Let us all try to make the world a more peaceful and better place to reside.

Why medicine?

Medicines are always an essential element to help individuals to alleviate from suffering as well as to improve the health quality of a society either back in ancient time or nowadays. Medicines are an indispensable element to human beings hence the supply of medicines in some countries sometimes may not be able to meet the demand especially when the country is suffering from a disaster. Millions of Indian don’t take their prescribed medication because they can’t afford it. With soaring copays, deductibles, and insurance costs, many people are making impossible choices between medications, food and housing, gas to get to work, etc.
However, not taking your medications often leads to even worse outcomes—heart attacks, strokes, and even higher costs. This is our nation's most critical problem, but it doesn't have to be. That's why we're here. We provide access, so everyone gets the care they deserve. I think abandoning people when they require help the most is the worst thing that a person can do. We don't want anyone to feel helpless, and this is what this website aims at.
